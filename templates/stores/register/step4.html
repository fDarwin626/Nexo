Nexo Seller Policy — Please Read

Subscription: You are renting a storefront on Nexo. Your subscription fee is non-refundable regardless of sales made.

Commission Structure:

  • Monthly revenue ₦0 – ₦500,000: 0% commission
  • Monthly revenue ₦500,001 – ₦2,000,000: 3% commission
  • Monthly revenue ₦2,000,001 – ₦10,000,000: 4% commission
  • Monthly revenue above ₦10,000,000: 5% commission (maximum)

Commission is automatically deducted via Flutterwave split payments before funds reach your account.

Outside Platform Transactions: Using Nexo-acquired customer contacts to conduct transactions outside the platform is a violation of this agreement and grounds for immediate account termination without subscription refund.

Disputes: Nexo reserves the right to suspend or terminate stores with repeated buyer complaints.
